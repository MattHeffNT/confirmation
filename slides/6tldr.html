Too long didn't read

aka "What's ya Phd on?"

  • I'm interested in what happens when you kind of mush together a few concepts and ideas.
  • I want to write about the ideas, technology, solutions or questions that emerge from said mushing. Or I should say synthesis.
  • I frame it like this... at the 1939 New York Wolrds Fair a fella called Norman Bel Geddes (dunno how to say his name) presented his vision of the future he called it Futurama (yes what the show is named after).
  • Futurama was funded by General Motors - it was a minature model of a city with highways and the promised freedom of cars. Through these imaginings, we now have the dystopia's of car centered cities. Deserts of Carparks. And a destructive fossil fuel industry that has its claws deep in politics and our psyche on what it means to be independent, successful and so on.
  • So what would happen if we built Futures, futuramas, cities that weren't "Enshitified". To borrow a term from Cory Doctorow. What would happen if they were Ochrepunked?
